How to Reach Us

A direct email or phone call is the fastest way to get your project moving.

For most facilities, a short email with the basics is enough to start. If your situation is urgent, call immediately and follow up with documents afterward.

Primary Contact Details

Email: andrew@haz3.com

Phone: (510) 935-5701

If you leave a voicemail, include your facility name, city, and a callback window.

What to include in your email

  • Facility name and city
  • Waste types (e.g., drums, bulk liquids, lab packs, universal waste)
  • Approximate quantities or count of containers
  • Requested timeframe for removal
  • Any access limits or scheduling constraints on-site

You do not need perfect profiles or every detail documented before reaching out. Send what you know and we will help structure the rest.
