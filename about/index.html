Our Story

Built out of day-to-day experience in hazardous waste operations, America Hazardous Waste Solutions focuses on the practical realities of moving waste safely off-site, not just theory.

Since 2012, our team has worked inside the hazardous waste industry supporting generators, transportation providers, and disposal facilities throughout Northern California. That experience showed a simple reality: most facilities do not need more complexity—they need a responsive point of contact that understands regulations, schedules, and budget constraints at the same time.

America Hazardous Waste Solutions was formed to close that gap. We coordinate compliant transportation and disposal options for drums, totes, bulk liquids, and solids, while keeping communication and documentation straightforward. The goal is to keep your waste moving and your operations focused on production, not paperwork.

Today, we support manufacturers, laboratories, utilities, construction firms, public agencies, and healthcare facilities across Solano, Napa, Sonoma, Contra Costa, Yolo, and Sacramento Counties—with a focus on responsiveness, clarity, and predictable follow-through.

How We Operate

How We Approach Every Project

  • Prioritize safety and regulatory alignment in every movement.
  • Coordinate around your production, outage, or shutdown windows.
  • Give clear expectations on timelines and logistics before arrival.

  • Support your recordkeeping with accurate manifests and documentation.

The objective is simple: waste off-site on time, with the right paperwork, and no surprises.

What It’s Like to Work With Us

One Point of Contact

You work through a single coordinator who understands your facility, recurring waste streams, and internal approval process. You don’t have to re-explain your operation every time you need a pickup.

Schedule-Driven Planning

Roles Behind the Work

Hazardous waste management is never a one-person job. Each role exists to keep movements safe, compliant, and on schedule.

Client Coordination

First point of contact for generators, responsible for understanding your waste streams, timelines, and internal approval structure, then translating that into an actionable plan.

Logistics & Scheduling

Aligns drivers, equipment, and disposal facility availability with your requested dates and on-site constraints so that loads move when you need them to move.

Documentation & Compliance Support

Focuses on profiles, manifests, and final disposal paperwork, helping your team maintain records that stand up to internal reviews and regulatory inspections.
