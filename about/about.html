About America Hazardous Waste Solutions

Founded in 2012, AHWS LLC was built to deliver safe, reliable, and well-documented hazardous waste transportation and disposal coordination throughout Northern California.

Our Story

America Hazardous Waste Solutions began as a transportation partner for industrial facilities that needed punctual service and consistent communication. Over the past decade, we have expanded to serve laboratories, healthcare, utilities, construction, and municipal clients who require dependable coordination across multiple waste streams.

Being headquartered in Solano County allows our team to reach the greater Bay Area and Sacramento corridor efficiently while maintaining close relationships with permitted treatment, storage, and disposal facilities.

Safety & Training

Safety culture is the foundation of our work. Drivers and handlers maintain current certifications, review route hazards before every pickup, and participate in refreshers on packaging, segregation, and emergency response communication.

We emphasize proper documentation and recordkeeping so that our customers can navigate inspections and audits with confidence.

Growth Timeline

2012 — Industry Launch

Entered the hazardous waste sector serving Solano County manufacturers with drum and tote transportation.

2015 — Expanded Capabilities

Added lab pack coordination, universal waste programs, and additional equipment to support complex pickups.

2018 — Regional Coverage

Established regular routes through Napa, Sonoma, Contra Costa, Yolo, and Sacramento Counties to support growing client demand.

2022 — Integrated Support

Enhanced documentation workflows, digital record sharing, and TSDF partnerships to streamline compliance for customers.
